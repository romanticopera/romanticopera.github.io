4 Trumpets & Trombones

Trumpets

The standard Romantic orchestra includes two or three trumpets. The first trumpet usually doubles the top note of the orchestra. If the orchestra includes three trumpets, then the second and third usually fill in the chord-tones directly below the first. Trombones

The standard Romantic orchestra includes three trombones plus an optional tuba or bass trombone. The tuba always plays the bass of the chord.

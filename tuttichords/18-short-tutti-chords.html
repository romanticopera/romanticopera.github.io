18 Short Tutti Chords

Short tutti chords abide by the same principles as long ones with the exception that chord-tones are less rigorously filled in. Short tutti chords often have a dry, staccato attack, which benefits from a lighter texture. This open spacing is especially common in rustic, folkish, or dance-like music.

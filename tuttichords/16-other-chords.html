16 Other Chords

The following chords are rarely employed in Romantic-era tutti chords, but must be included for completeness’ sake.

Augmented Sixth Chords

Augmented sixth chords follow the same rules as seventh chords in two ways: (1) the bass note is often omitted from the higher registers, and (2) the interval of a major second is avoided in the highest register when it would involve the voice.
